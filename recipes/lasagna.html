Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Each layer blends perfectly to create a hearty and comforting meal that everyone loves.This homemade version is baked to golden perfection, with soft pasta sheets soaked in savory sauce and gooey cheese — perfect for family dinners or special occasions.
